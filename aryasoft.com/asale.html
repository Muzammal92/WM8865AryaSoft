For Better and More Effective Sales Management

Smart Sales Management and Tracking System - an intelligent tool to enhance your sales management process.

ASALE is designed to help businesses maximize sales productivity, ASALE simplifies work processes for all parties involved in the selling cycle. It also provides intelligence on sales activities, prospects and customers for better planning and management.

Dashboard allows executives to see different aspect of important information in a single page, anywhere anytime.

Launching ASALE, all your upcoming activities will be displayed on your calendar, with contact information, phone numbers and meeting locations. ASALE reminds you of your next visits so that you will never miss an appointment.

A ‘check-in and check-out’ function allows salespersons to log their activities, which will help them complete sales reports with ease, while managers can track and monitor their team efficiently.

Real-time quotation allows salespersons to choose products, fill-in the quantity being sold, offer a discount and create a quotation on the spot. All the information can be sent to the back-office and processed accordingly, reducing time and travelling cost.
